About Fancy Foods
Wholesale Nuts & Brokerage Services

Fancy Foods supplies roasted nuts and seeds to shops and wholesalers, and also works as a trusted broker connecting food traders with buyers and suppliers of rice, starch, coffee, spices and other dry commodities.

Who we are

Fancy Foods began as a specialized supplier of nuts, seeds and roasted snacks: pistachios, mixed nuts, sunflower seeds and crunchy corn. As our network grew, we expanded into brokerage for food traders.

What we do

  • Supply nuts & roasted snacks for shops and wholesalers.
  • Act as a broker between serious food traders.
  • Help both sides clarify quantity, quality and delivery terms.
  • Follow deals until completion and commission settlement.

Brokerage Services

We help traders find the right buyer or supplier for bulk food commodities.

Products we cover

  • Rice (origins & qualities)
  • Starch & flours
  • Coffee & related goods
  • Spices & blends
  • Other dry items upon request

How a deal works

  1. Trader contacts us with product + target price
  2. We shortlist serious partners
  3. We coordinate the negotiation
  4. Terms are written and confirmed
  5. Commission is paid after successful completion

Why traders choose us

  • We filter serious offers
  • We reduce misunderstandings
  • Transparent communication
  • Fast coordination
